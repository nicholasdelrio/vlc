Dust storm off Mauritania and Western Sahara
Pictured on September 14, 2013, from the MODIS instrument on the Aqua satellite. Visit LANCE for more near real-time data products from EOSDIS.
DAAC Story of the Week - Sampling the Submesoscale Ocean in the Santa Barbara Channel
NASAs Surface Water Ocean Topography (SWOT) satellite, expected to launch in 2020, will measure sea level height variations on spatial scales down to a few kilometers. This mission will ultimately allow the oceans submesoscale (spatial scales from kilometers to a few 10s of kilometers) variations to be observed from space. Visit the Physical Oceanography DAAC (PO.DAAC) for more information.
North American Forest Dynamics Project: Forest Disturbance and Regrowth Data
Location of the Eastern and Western sample sites for which Landsat Time Series Stacks (STSS) were assembled, from NACP North American Forest Dynamics Project: Forest Disturbance and Regrowth Data. Visit the Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL) for more information.
SEDAC Image of the Week - Global Population Density, 2010
Global Population Density, 2010. Taken from the Population, Landscape and Climate Estimates, v3.0 collection at SEDAC. Visit the SEDAC Map Gallery for more mapping products from EOSDIS.
